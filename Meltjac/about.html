This business was started in the year 2008 by Linus Juma Bwire as a humble garbage disposal service around Kayole and Komarock area serving a few flats. The business grew and by 19th March,2014 was registered by the business name Meltjac Environmental Service.The name M.E.L.T.J.A.C actually is an acronym for the family members names as follows M-Michael,E-Emmanuel,L-Linus,T-Teresa,J-Joseph(Father),A-Austin,C-Consolota(maiden mother).The business is registered under the number BN/2014/276062 under the Registration of business names acts as per the Kenyan Constitution.Soon the business is upgrading to a limited company as it abides by the adage "Rome was not built in one day".
